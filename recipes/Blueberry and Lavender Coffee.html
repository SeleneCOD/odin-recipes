Blueberry and Lavender Coffee

This Blueberry and Lavender Coffee recipe was something I came up with while on my search for coffee creamer at Trader Joe's.I have always loved the taste of lavender in my coffee, but when I saw this beverage contained blueberries, I had to give it a try! The lady at the check out counter even gave me the thumbs up, she said it was delicious. Now, I want to share my recipe for the world to try.

For this recipe we will use 1 double shot of espresso, but feel free to use fresh brewed coffee. Either way you make it, it is going to be good! It can be served hot or iced.

Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's Blueberry Lavender Flavored Almond Beverage, 7-8 oz
  • 1 double shot (2 oz) of espresso (or strong coffee)
  • Optional: Sugar

Steps

  1. Brew and mix :

    2. Brew espresso and gently mix into a mug with Trader Joe's Blueberry Lavender Flavored Almond Beverage.

  2. Optional: Add Sugar.

    3. You may add sugar to taste if coffee is not sweet enough.

  3. Serve!
